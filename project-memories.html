H.C.A.T.M.W.A.T.F

Here Come All The Memories With All Their Forgettings

FOCUS

  • Research
  • Fictional futures

OUTPUT

  • Project Room
  • Fiction narratives
  • Multimedia devices

The main theme of this project revolves around false memories, coupled with the creation of fiction that could critically reconsider our position as students at the Faculty of Fine Arts, University of Lisbon. This reflection is made possible through the crafting of a fictional narrative, materialized in the creation of a persona (Camila) in three stages of her life. The speculative scenario is transposed into our reality through audio elements, visual components (videos and physical objects), and digital aspects (Camilaâ€™s computer files). All elements complement each other to function in synchrony within a carefully designed Project Room experience, lasting for 15 minutes.

Why are we proud of it?

H.C.A.T.M.W.A.T.F was a very complete and multifaceted project. Besides thorough research on a very complex topic, we had to create an immersive experience. This demanded the creation of a detailed narrative that revolved around the future of student designers through 3 different timelines. To bring this story and persona to life, we developed an experience in a physical space with physical objects synced with 3 videos and 3 different audios that had a written script read by 3 different AI voices.

Overall, this project had a lot of devices, objects, and nuances that really came to life when put together. You can see all about it on the project website.
